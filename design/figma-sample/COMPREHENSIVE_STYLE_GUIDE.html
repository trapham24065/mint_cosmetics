1.3 Logo & Iconography

Logo Usage

  • Always maintain minimum space around logo (1x logo height)
  • Use on light backgrounds (primary) or dark backgrounds (white variant - to be created)
  • Minimum size: 40x40px on web, 44x44pt on mobile

Icon Guidelines

  • Icon sizes: 16px, 20px, 24px, 32px, 48px
  • Stroke width: 2px for consistency
  • Corners: 2px border-radius for softness
  • Use primary color (#FF6565) for active/selected icons
  • Use neutral-500 (#9E9E9E) for inactive icons

1.4 Imagery & Illustration Style

Photography style:

  • Natural, clean lighting with soft shadows
  • Focus on product texture and quality
  • Neutral or white backgrounds preferred
  • Aspect ratio: 1:1 for product cards, 16:9 for hero images

Illustration style:

  • Minimalist, flat design with subtle gradients
  • Color palette: use brand colors (primary, secondary, neutrals)
  • Stroke-based icons with 2px weight
  • Used for: empty states, onboarding, feature highlights

4. Quy tắc tương tác (Interaction & Behavior)

4.1 Animation & Transition

Timing & Duration

  • Fast: 100-150ms — micro interactions (button hover, icon change)
  • Normal: 200-300ms — standard transitions (fade, slide)
  • Slow: 300-500ms — modal/drawer opening, complex animations

Easing Functions

  • ease-in-out: Smooth entering and exiting (default)
  • ease-in: Accelerating animations (moving off-screen)
  • ease-out: Decelerating animations (appearing on-screen)
  • linear: Constant speed (progress bars, loading spinners)
